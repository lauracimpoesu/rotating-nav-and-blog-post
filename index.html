Why Everybody Loves Golden Retrievers

by Laura Cimpoesu

Goldens have something special when it comes to canine healing arts and has chosen the breed, all AKC registered. Their calming aura is warms all hearts. We call them a comfort rug with a heartbeat. Goldens are great listeners, they show unconditional love, they’re confidential, they don’t take notes and they’re nonjudgmental. On top of that, they are versatile, able to function in all kinds of situations, with people ranging from young children to elderly Alzheimer’s patients. They can master many skills, and will work for multiple handlers. Their quick minds—and background as gundogs—allow them to concentrate in the face of distractions encountered at disaster sites—sirens, shouting, even with guns or other loud noises in the background. And, of course, there’s one other, most important factor: Goldens, by nature, are lovers.

Meet Lulu, my precious Goldie

My beautiful Golden Retriever is the sweetest dog ever. Her name is Lulu Princess and she is 2 years old (young). She loves to play with multiple toys, go on walks and have lots of fun with mommie and daddy. She enjoys playing with other dogs and people as well. She's an incredibly social and loving Golden Retriever, just like pretty much they all are. I sincerely believe this is one of the best dog breeds ever. Golden Retrievers are a gift from God.
